Graham Rutherford

Masters Student, 2020 - 2021

Graham received his degree in Media & Information(TV, Cinema, & Radio) in 2017 from Michigan State University. Hes always enjoyed working on sets or in the studio, but was always curious about the logistical side of media and media design. He came to this program from Michigan because he wanted to use his current media skills along with what he learns about design theory to make a difference in various social justice issues.

Graham gained a basic knowledge of video editing and photo retouching from his undergraduate degree. He wants to learn how to use them more for creating designs that have a positive impact and benefit any community hes in. Hed like to improve his media literacy and art skills so his "toolset" can be more diverse. Hed like to take what hell learn through this program to go work at a non-profit or a community-based group.

civic scholars.civic designers.civic mediamakers.
